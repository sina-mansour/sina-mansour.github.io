This invited talk provided an introduction to lifespan normative modeling, focusing on charting healthy norms and detecting pathological deviations across the human lifespan.

The talk covered methodologies for constructing normative models that capture typical developmental trajectories and highlighted their applications in identifying deviations associated with various neurological and psychiatric conditions. This talk aimed to foster potential institutional collaborations in advancing lifespan normative modeling research across multiple cohorts and modalities.
