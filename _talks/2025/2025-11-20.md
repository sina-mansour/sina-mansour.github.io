This invited talk provided and introduction to various aspects of open science in academia and explored practical steps to get started with implementing best practices for open science.

The talk covered topics such as data sharing, open access publishing, pre-registration of studies, and the use of open-source tools for data analysis. Attendees were guided through actionable strategies to incorporate open science principles into their research workflows, with an emphasis on enhancing transparency, reproducibility, and collaboration within the scientific community.

The slides for this talk are available [here](https://sina-mansour.github.io/systems_lab_open_science_presentation_2025/).
