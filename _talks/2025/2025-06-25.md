This featured talk (part of the oral session on the Disorders of the Nervous System), along with a poster presentation ([available online](https://github.com/sina-mansour/OHBM_2025/tree/main/SNM_poster)) explores the clinical applications of our novel spectral normative modeling approach for high-resolution brain abnormality inference.

This poster was awarded the **OHBM 2025 Merit Award** .
